Caribbean Cruise

Christmas 2016

The cruise was with the Viking Ocean Cruises. Named the West Indies Explorer sailing from San Juan, Pureto Rico to Tortola, Antigua, St Lucia, Barbados, Guadeloupe, St Kitts and Nevis, St Thomas and returning to San Juan. It took 10 days. The ship itself is not the largest sailing in the Caribbean but still large enough both in length and height.
