    • All the islands visited apart from Tortola which was called off due to windy conditions appear to be very similar in nature. All had very green vegetation. In all the ship was able to berth virtually in the town with very little dock to the main streets. All islands apart from Barbados were very hilly. The roads depended on the island visited as to what condition they were in. All experienced some form of traffic congestion.

    The main currency which was accepted throughout was the American dollar. There is the East Caribben Dollar but it is easier to use the USA dollar.
